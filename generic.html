Weight-for-age z-scores (WAZ)

Welcome to this specific exploration within the R Script section of my portfolio. This project delves into studying the predictors of weight in Romanian infants, employing the Weight-for-age z-scores (WAZ) as a primary metric. Through a series of well-structured R scripts, I have analyzed a dataset encompassing various demographic factors such as mother's location, marital status, socio-economic status, and age, as well as infants' birth term, age, anemia status, rank in the family, and dietary patterns.

In this section, you will find the R scripts used for data analysis alongside a thorough explanation of the methodologies implemented, key findings, and visualizations that were generated to better understand the underlying relationships. This project exemplifies my ability to utilize R for rigorous data analysis and statistical modeling, aiming to unearth significant insights that could potentially inform better healthcare strategies for infant populations.

Feel free to explore the scripts listed below, along with the accompanying narrative that details each step of the analysis journey. I warmly welcome any collaborative inquiries or discussions stemming from this work.
